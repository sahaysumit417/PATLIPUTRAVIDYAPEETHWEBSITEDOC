🏫 About Our School

Welcome to Patliputra Vidyapeeth

Patliputra Vidyapeeth runs under Manava manavi samaj kalyan kendra, Patna, a philanthropic voluntary organisation, registered under the Societies Registration Act-1860. In pursuit of its objective of Human Resource Development, it resolved to set up a School of selective basic education.

Students, particularly from rural poor families, are taught and trained here in a socio-rural environment cutting across the barriers of rural and urban areas, alleviating the root of inequalities incidental to rich and poor, caste and class, and other related circumstances.

OUR Mission

The mission of Patliputra Vidyapeeth is to ensure sound infrastructural facilities in the field of education, education & research. Our aim is to provide quality health education for qualified professional support to the Medical and Rehab professional.

Our Aim and Objectives

We know children are the future of the nation on whom the major role of shaping out the future depends. So, our aim is to make children prosperous and worthy citizens of the nation by dint of a basic and systematic way of education and providing them a sound environment in overall development under the care of highly motivated, trained, and dedicated teachers.

Our Vision

"Teaching is the noblest profession of the world." We believe that the teaching-learning process should be a process of discovery. Our school ensures both physical and mental growth of the students through training.

Education means an all round development of a child. Teacher is a philosopher, guide and a friend. Indeed, we have experienced and qualified teachers who dedicate their life for this noble cause. Patliputra Vidyapeeth inculcates the spirit of patriotism, sharing, caring, cooperation, and coordination.

"शिक्षण दुनिया का सबसे बेहतरीन पेशा है" हमारा मानना है कि शिक्षण-सीखने की प्रक्रिया खोज की प्रक्रिया होना चाहिए। हमारे स्कूल प्रशिक्षण के माध्यम से छात्रों के शारीरिक और मानसिक विकास दोनों को सुनिश्चित करता है।

शिक्षा का अर्थ है कि एक बच्चे का एक समस्त विकास हो। टीचर एक दार्शनिक, गाइड और एक मित्र है। दरअसल, हमारे पास अनुभवी और योग्य शिक्षक हैं जो इस महान कार्य के लिए अपनी जिंदगी को समर्पित करते हैं। पाटलिपुत्रा विद्यापीठ देशभक्ति की भावना, देखभाल, सहयोग और समन्वय साझा करते हैं।
